## Observations and Mock Limitations

### What's working well

1. **Geography gating** — Scotland and Greater Manchester correctly hard-fail. Kent areas get the 1.10x boost. South East gets 1.05x. UK-wide gets no boost.
2. **Funding value scoring** — Correctly reflects M4W's sweet spots. The 15k-30k band gets probability=6 (matching their 62% historical rate), while 30k+ gets probability=2.
3. **Effort detection** — "Expression of interest", "short form", "one-page" correctly trigger high effort scores (easy applications). "Theory of change", "business plan", "detailed proposal" correctly trigger low effort scores.
4. **Tag generation** — Quick Win, Strong Match, High Value, and Multi-Year tags all fire at appropriate thresholds.
5. **Gating soft vs hard** — Previously-applied and low extraction confidence correctly produce needs_review (still scored) rather than hard fails.
6. **Relative ranking** — Top 3 (Henry Smith, Sisters of the Holy Cross, Allen Lane) are exactly the kind of grants M4W wins. Bottom scored entries (Dover small grant, tech startup) are correctly de-prioritised.

### Known mock limitations (will improve in Phase 2)

1. **Strategic fit is purely keyword-based** — Funders whose descriptions don't use M4W vocabulary (like The Fore, test-017) score low despite being excellent matches. LLM will understand context.
2. **Probability ignores funder relationship** — The mock only looks at grant size band. LLM will consider that existing funders (Henry Smith, Allen Lane) have higher reapplication success rates.
3. **Effort is coarse** — Only detects specific phrases. Many grants don't mention their process in the description. LLM can infer from funder type and grant size.
4. **"High Value" tag can be misleading** — It fires on funding_value >= 9 regardless of alignment (see test-009, the tech grant). Phase 2 could add a strategic_fit floor to this tag.
5. **Eligibility gating is too permissive** — Any single keyword match passes. "Community garden project" would pass even if it has nothing to do with M4W's specific work. LLM will do proper mission alignment assessment.
